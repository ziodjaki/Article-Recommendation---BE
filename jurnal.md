# Scientechno: Journal of Science and Technology

Focus and Scope
Focus:
Scientechno: Journal of Science and Technology focuses on interdisciplinary research that bridges fundamental science and advanced technology to build systemic, intelligent, and sustainable solutions for future resilience. The journal prioritizes studies that integrate computational, ecological, and engineering perspectives to address complex challenges in energy, health, education, environment, and digital transformation.

Scope:

The journal welcomes theoretical and applied research in the following areas:

Computational Science for Natural System Modeling
Modeling and simulation of complex systems using AI and data science to understand and predict natural phenomena such as climate patterns, biodiversity loss, and disease outbreaks.

Techno-Ecological Innovation
Development of bioinspired and eco-friendly technologies for environmental restoration, green energy, smart agriculture, and ecological risk mitigation.

Systemic AI and Cyber-Physical Technologies
Design of intelligent and adaptive systems integrating physical infrastructure with digital intelligence in fields such as smart health, autonomous systems, and Industry 5.0.

Resilient and Adaptive Learning Technologies
Educational technologies that enhance learning adaptability, equity, and resilience in formal and informal contexts, including AI-based learning analytics, gamification, and mobile learning.

Circular Technology and Low-Waste Engineering
Innovation in circular design, sustainable material engineering, molecular recycling, and low-carbon solutions for a regenerative and resource-efficient future.

# Lingeduca: Journal of Language and Education Studies

Focus and Scope
Focus
Lingeduca: Journal of Language and Education Studies focuses on interdisciplinary research in language, literacy, and education that promotes transformative learning, intercultural understanding, and social justice. The journal prioritizes applied and critical studies of language practices, language education, and literacies across diverse social, cultural, and technological contexts. It seeks innovative scholarship that challenges dominant paradigms and proposes pedagogical alternatives aligned with global and local educational challenges.

Scope
The journal welcomes empirical and theoretical manuscripts in the following areas:

Applied and Critical Language Studies
Language and identity, multilingual education, intercultural communication, and language use in marginalized communities.

Technology-Enhanced Language and Literacy Education
AI in language teaching, gamified learning, augmented reality, digital storytelling, and mobile-assisted language learning.

Multilingual and Multicultural Education
Pedagogical strategies and language policy for linguistically and culturally diverse classrooms.

Language, Literacy, and Social Justice
Critical literacies, inclusive practices, gender and linguistic equity, and the politics of language in education.

Curriculum and Assessment in Language Education
Competency-based curriculum design, digital formative assessment, interactive portfolios, and language teacher development.

# Sharia Oikonomia Law Journal

Focus and Scope
Sharia Oikonomia Law Journal is a peer-reviewed academic journal that focuses on the intersection of Islamic economic principles, contemporary legal frameworks, and global economic challenges. The journal provides a platform for innovative research and critical discourse on the following specialized areas:

Digital Transformation in Sharia Economic Law: Examining the impact of digital technologies (e.g., fintech, blockchain, AI) on Islamic finance, including regulatory challenges, Sharia compliance, and dispute resolution in digital economies.

Sustainable Development and Islamic Economic Law: Exploring the role of Islamic financial instruments (e.g., zakat, waqf, sukuk) in achieving sustainable development goals (SDGs), with a focus on green finance, social welfare, and environmental sustainability.

Global Governance and Sharia Economic Law: Investigating the harmonization of Sharia economic principles with international legal standards, cross-border dispute resolution, and the global integration of Islamic finance.

Behavioral Economics and Islamic Business Ethics: Analyzing decision-making processes, ethical frameworks, and behavioral insights within Islamic business and financial practices.

Islamic Social Finance and Inclusive Economic Systems: Highlighting innovative models of zakat, waqf, and Islamic microfinance to address poverty, inequality, and financial inclusion.

Sharia Compliance in Emerging Markets: Addressing the challenges and opportunities of implementing Sharia-compliant economic systems in non-Muslim majority countries and emerging markets.

Islamic Economic Law and Crisis Management: Studying the role of Islamic financial instruments in mitigating and recovering from economic, health, and environmental crises.

Comparative Studies on Sharia Economic Law: Offering comparative analyses between Sharia economic law and other legal systems (e.g., common law, civil law) to identify synergies and divergences in practice and regulation.

The journal welcomes interdisciplinary research, empirical studies, and theoretical frameworks that contribute to the advancement of knowledge in these areas. By addressing contemporary issues and fostering global dialogue, Sharia Oikonomia Law Journal aims to become a leading reference for scholars, practitioners, and policymakers in the field of Islamic economic law.

# Journal Neosantara Hybrid Learning

Focus and Scope
Journal Neosantara Hybrid Learning is an journal dedicated to original, innovative, and critical studies related to the integration of advanced technology in hybrid learning practices, emphasizing multiculturalism, social inclusion, and the development of adaptive pedagogy. Journal Neosantara Hybrid Learning encourages the publication of research articles, theoretical studies, instructional design developments, and critical evaluations regarding the implementation of digital technologies in various educational settings, including formal, informal, and non-formal contexts.

The specific scope of the journal includes:

Hybrid learning within multicultural educational contexts.

Inclusive educational technology and universal design for learning (UDL).

Integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and predictive analytics for personalized hybrid learning.

Ethical issues, data privacy, and security in digitally-based hybrid learning.

Gamification and immersive technologies (virtual, augmented, and mixed reality) in the design and implementation of hybrid learning.

Adaptation and management of hybrid education in emergency and global crisis situations.

Integration of social-emotional learning (SEL) and digital wellbeing in hybrid education.

Development of hybrid pedagogies and enhancement of teachers' professional competencies through digital technology.

Emerging trends in neuroeducation applied to hybrid learning design.

Application of blockchain technologies and decentralized systems in hybrid education administration and management.

Journal Neosantara Hybrid Learning is committed to becoming a leading platform for academics, researchers, practitioners, and educational technology developers to publish impactful work advancing educational development and innovation in the digital era.

# Journal of World Future Medicine, Health and Nursing

Focus & Scope
Focus:
This journal focuses on research and innovations related to the future of medicine, global health, and nursing. The primary focus of this journal is the exploration of cutting-edge medical technologies, innovative preventive health approaches, and the development of sustainable and adaptive nursing care models in response to social, economic, and environmental changes. The journal aims to showcase research that offers new insights into how medical and healthcare systems can be transformed to address future global health challenges.

Scope:
This journal includes research articles and scientific reviews on the following topics:

Future Medical Technologies: Research on the latest innovations in medical technology, including artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, telemedicine, nano-medicine, and the development of genetic-based diagnostic and therapeutic technologies.
Future Global Health and Epidemiology: Exploration of infectious and non-infectious diseases predicted to pose future global health threats, and strategies for prevention and control through technology-based solutions.
Technology-Integrated Nursing: Studies on the integration of technology into nursing practice, such as the use of wearable devices, AI in patient management, and tele-nursing to enhance the efficiency and accessibility of care.
Preventive Health and Health Promotion: Research on innovative approaches to health promotion, preventive lifestyle interventions, and community-based solutions to reduce the future burden of diseases, as well as disease prevention strategies, community-based health interventions, and managing both communicable and non-communicable diseases.
Impact of Social and Environmental Changes on Health: Research highlighting the effects of climate change, urbanization, and social dynamics on human health, and scientifically-based strategies for adaptation.
Sustainable Healthcare Models: The development of sustainable healthcare and nursing models focused on inclusivity, universal access to healthcare services, especially in underserved areas.
Ethics and Future Health Policy: Discussions on ethical challenges and policy considerations regarding the use of advanced technologies in medicine and nursing, and their impact on healthcare accessibility.
Applied Clinical Research: Development and evaluation of innovative medical treatments, clinical trials, new therapies, and diagnostic approaches that have a direct impact on patient care.
Healthcare Management: Optimization of healthcare systems, quality improvement in service delivery, hospital management, and the application of health informatics in clinical practice.
Evidence-Based Nursing: Patient-centered care approaches, palliative care, pain management, and improving the quality of life for patients in various clinical settings.
Education and Professional Development: Research on the training and education of healthcare professionals, and the development of competencies in evidence-based clinical practice.
Interdisciplinary Collaboration: Studies on the importance of collaboration between physicians, nurses, pharmacists, and other healthcare professionals to provide holistic care to patients.
This journal accepts quantitative and qualitative research articles, case studies, and literature reviews that provide new insights into future developments in medicine, global health, and nursing.

# Pengabdian: Jurnal Abdimas

Focus & Scope
Pengabdian: Jurnal Abdimas is a scientific journal dedicated to publishing innovative and impactful community service outcomes. The journal focuses on the following topics:

Community Empowerment through Digital Technology: Exploring how digital technology can be utilized to enhance the welfare and empowerment of local communities.

Development of Social Entrepreneurship in Remote Communities: Studies on social entrepreneurship initiatives that have successfully improved the economy and welfare in remote areas.

Preservation of Local Culture through Community Service Programs: Research on efforts to preserve and promote local culture through community service activities.

Community-Based Health Education for Disease Prevention: Programs that involve communities in the prevention and management of diseases through health education.

Community-Based Environmental Management: Initiatives that involve communities in the conservation and management of local natural resources.

Sustainable Agricultural Innovations in Empowering Local Farmers: Application of sustainable agricultural technologies and practices to improve the productivity of local farmers.

Development of Inclusive Education through Community Engagement: Efforts to create inclusive educational environments with active community participation.

Utilization of Renewable Energy by Local Communities: Projects that involve communities in the development and use of renewable energy sources.

Capacity Building of Civil Society Organizations: Training and assistance to enhance the effectiveness of community organizations in achieving their social goals.

The Role of Arts and Creativity in Community Empowerment: Initiatives that use arts and creative activities to empower and unify communities.

The journal accepts articles that present the implementation of community service programs, including case studies, program reports, and impact evaluations related to the above topics.
